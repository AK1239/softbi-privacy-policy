Introduction

Soft BI ("the App") is a business intelligence mobile application developed for authorized users of TCB Bank. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.
2

Who We Are

The App is operated by Softnet on behalf of TCB Bank. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at:

  • Email: info@softnet.co.tz
3

Information We Collect

a) Authentication Information

When you log in, the App uses Keycloak OAuth 2.0 with PKCE (OpenID Connect) to authenticate you through your organization's identity provider. We collect:

  • Access tokens, refresh tokens, and ID tokens provided by the identity server
  • Basic profile information returned by the identity server (name, email address) via the openid, profile, and email OAuth scopes
We do not collect or store your password. Authentication is handled entirely by your organization's Keycloak identity server.

b) Usage Preferences

The App stores the following locally on your device using SharedPreferences:

  • Theme mode preference (light / dark)
  • API server configuration URL

c) Dashboard & Business Data

The App displays business intelligence data (charts, KPIs, reports) fetched from your organization's Apache Superset instance. This data is read-only within the App and is not transmitted anywhere other than back to your organization's servers.

4

How We Use Your Information

Data Purpose
Authentication tokens To verify your identity and maintain your session
User profile (name, email) To personalize your in-app experience
Theme preference To remember your display settings between sessions
Server configuration To connect to your organization's BI backend
We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the App's core functionality.
5

Data Storage

  • On-device: Authentication tokens and preferences are stored locally on your device using encrypted SharedPreferences.
  • Organizational servers: All business intelligence data resides on your organization's servers (Keycloak and Apache Superset). The App only retrieves and displays this data; it does not store BI data locally beyond in-memory caching during your session.
  • No third-party cloud storage: We do not send your data to any third-party cloud services.
6

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Data is exchanged only between the App and your organization's own servers:

  • Keycloak identity server (tcbbank.co.tz) — for authentication
  • Apache Superset — for fetching business intelligence data
7

Third-Party Services & SDKs

The App uses the following third-party libraries. None of them collect personal data independently:

8

Session Management

  • Authentication tokens are stored locally and used to maintain your session.
  • When you log out, all tokens are cleared from the device and an end-session request is sent to the Keycloak server.
  • If your session expires, the App will automatically attempt a token refresh. If the refresh fails, you will be redirected to the login screen.
9

Data Retention

  • Authentication tokens are retained on-device until you log out or uninstall the App.
  • Preferences (theme, server URL) are retained until you uninstall the App or manually clear app data.
  • Business data is held only in memory during your active session and is not persisted to disk.
10

Security

  • Authentication uses OAuth 2.0 Authorization Code flow with PKCE, an industry-standard secure authentication protocol.
  • All communication with servers uses HTTPS/TLS.
  • We do not store passwords or sensitive credentials.
11

Children's Privacy

The App is intended exclusively for authorized business users of TCB Bank. It is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data held about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent

Since most data is managed by your organization's identity provider (Keycloak), please contact your organization's IT / data team for data access or deletion requests.
